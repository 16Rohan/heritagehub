Signature Art Forms

Blue Pottery

A unique Turko-Persian art form using a special quartz dough, famed for its brilliant cobalt blue dye.

Bandhani

An ancient tie-dye technique creating intricate patterns, symbolizing the vibrant culture of the region.

Meenakari

The exquisite art of enameling precious metals with vibrant colors, traditionally used in royal jewelry.
